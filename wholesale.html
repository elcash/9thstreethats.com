Carrying 9th Street Hats in your store

If you would like to carry 9th Street Hats in your store or need to order in bulk for your ensemble or theater group, you may contact us to place your order.

Minimum orders

Most of our hats have minimums of 12 pieces per order, but some specialty items require only 6 pieces. Our company is willing to work with you to please your customers, so be sure to ask about the details of our "swap-out" program. You may always request the sizes and quantities that you need.

Catalog Request

If you would like a catalog and order sheet, please contact our parent company, Levine Hat Company, at 1-800-875-1428. You may also request a catalog by filling out this contact form.
